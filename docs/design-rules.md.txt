# Design Rules

## Visual Direction

Premium.
Elegant.
Modern.
Clean.

The website must never look:

- cheap
- aggressive
- spam-like
- discount focused

## Layout

Whitespace is important.

Do not overcrowd sections.

Avoid unnecessary visual elements.

## Typography

Keep existing typography system.

Do not introduce random fonts.

## Colors

Maintain existing color palette.

Do not introduce additional accent colors.

## CTA Buttons

CTA buttons must remain clearly visible.

Primary CTA:

- Phone call
- Booking

## Mobile

No horizontal scrolling.

Buttons must remain easily tappable.

Text must remain readable.

Spacing must remain comfortable.
