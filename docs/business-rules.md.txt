# Business Rules

## Primary Goal

Generate business opportunities.

## Priority Order

1. Phone calls
2. Booking requests
3. Contact form submissions
4. Service inquiries
5. Blog engagement

## Positioning

Premium service.

Not discount service.

Not price-focused.

Not coupon-oriented.

## Communication Style

Professional.
Trustworthy.
Expert.
Clear.

## Trust Building

Always prioritize:

- expertise
- quality
- experience
- customer confidence

over aggressive sales messaging.

## Content Strategy

Educational content is important.

However:

Educational content must support conversions.

The website is not a magazine.

The website is a business tool.

## Decision Rule

When choosing between:

- visual effect
- conversion

always prioritize conversion.
