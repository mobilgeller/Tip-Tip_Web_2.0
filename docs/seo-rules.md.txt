# AI SEO Rules

## Objective

Content must be understandable by:

- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Gemini
- Perplexity
- future AI systems

## Content Structure

Prefer:

- clear headings
- concise answers
- FAQ sections
- structured explanations

## Writing Style

Clear.
Factual.
Specific.

Avoid unnecessary marketing language.

## Expertise

Demonstrate practical expertise.

Prefer real examples.

Prefer problem-solution explanations.

## Authority

Support claims with:

- practical experience
- real-world examples
- technical reasoning

## Content Goal

Become a trusted answer source for industrial cleaning and automotive detailing related topics.
